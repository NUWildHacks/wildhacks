ABOUT

WildHacks is Chicago's largest intercollegiate hackathon. This November, 500 of the most talented students from the Midwest and around the country will come to Northwestern to ideate, develop, and launch novel hardware and software projects. In just 24 hours, learn from top industry mentors, collaborate with amazing teammates, and turn your wildest ideas into reality.
