FAQ

How do I connect to the wifi?

You can connect to the Northwestern network by providing the temporary NetID and password found on your wristband as login credentials.

Where can I go to sleep?

The Evans Room is reserved for sleeping space. Additional sleeping space will be in the Starbucks lounge after 12pm and until 10am.

Whom can I ask technical questions?

Mentors are here to help you with any tech questions you might have. If you'd like to submit a request for a mentor to help you with something, please visit the HELPq.

Where can I get something to eat?

Meals will be served on the ground floor of Norris. You can find out when meals are by looking at the schedule.

How do I submit my submission?

You'll need to create a Devpost account in order to submit projects. Once you do, submissions can be uploaded here.

How are submissions judged?

When is the deadline for submissions?

Hacking ends with a hard deadline of 11:30am. Submissions provided after 11:30 will not be accepted.

What if I have another question?

If you have more questions, feel free to flag down a WildHacks team member or volunteer in a coral shirt.
